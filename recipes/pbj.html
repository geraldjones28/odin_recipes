Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description:

A classic American culinary creation consisting of two slices of bread, typically white or wheat, spread with peanut butter on one slice and jelly or jam on the other. When assembled, the slices are pressed together to form a simple yet delicious sandwich beloved by people of all ages. The combination of creamy peanut butter and sweet, fruity jelly or jam creates a satisfying blend of flavors and textures, making it a popular choice for lunches, snacks, or quick meals.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices sandwich bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons grape jelly or 2 teaspoons strawberry jam

Steps:

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other side.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich.
  4. Toddler adaptation: cut off crusts before serving.
